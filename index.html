Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app for?

▼

The Universal Schedule Creator is a flexible employee scheduling tool designed to work for any business type—hotels, restaurants, retail stores, healthcare facilities, and more. It helps you organize employee availability, generate schedules, and enforce custom staffing rules tailored to your specific business needs.

Key Features

▶
  • Employee Management: Add, edit, and remove employees with custom job titles
  • Availability Tracking: Set 7-day work schedules with specific shift times
  • Schedule Generation: Create 1-4 week schedules automatically based on availability
  • Custom Validation Rules: Define your own staffing requirements (minimum staff per day, etc.)
  • Job Title Grouping: Organize employees and schedules by department or role
  • Visual Indicators: Color-coded availability and rule violation highlighting
  • Test Data Generator: Quickly populate with sample employees for demo purposes

What size business is this ideal for?

▶

This tool is best suited for small to medium-sized businesses with approximately 1-50 employees. It's perfect for businesses that need flexible scheduling without the complexity and cost of enterprise software. For teams larger than 50 employees, you may experience performance limitations with local storage and manual schedule management.

Important Limitations

▶
  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored in your browser's local storage. There is no cloud backup or synchronization.
  • Single Device: Data does not transfer between different devices or browsers. Use the same device and browser consistently.
  • No Auto-Backup: Clear your browser data = lose all schedules. Consider taking screenshots or manual backups.
  • Manual Scheduling: Schedules must be manually generated and edited. There is no automatic scheduling optimization.
  • No Export (Yet): Currently no built-in PDF or CSV export functionality.

How do I use the Rules System?

▶

Click "Manage Rules" in the navbar to create custom validation rules. For example, you can create a rule requiring "Minimum 3 Housekeepers per day" which will check your generated schedule and highlight any dates that don't meet this requirement.

Steps:

  1. Click "Manage Rules" and then "Add New Rule"
  2. Give your rule a descriptive name
  3. Select the job title it applies to
  4. Choose the condition type (currently minCountPerDay, more coming soon)
  5. Set the threshold (e.g., "3" for minimum 3 employees)
  6. Choose alert level: Warning (yellow) or Error (red)
  7. Save the rule

When you generate a schedule, any rule violations will be displayed with colored warnings and highlighted date columns.

How do I get started?

▶
  1. Click "Add Employee" to create your first employee
  2. Enter job title, name, and select available work days with shift times
  3. Repeat for all employees
  4. (Optional) Create validation rules via "Manage Rules"
  5. Click "Generate Schedule" to create a weekly or multi-week schedule
  6. Review any rule violations and adjust employee availability as needed

Tip: Use "Generate Test Data" to quickly populate the app with sample employees to explore features.
